Black Hat Visualization

Bar chart showing stock market closing price of Netflix

Reasons why it's misleading..

  1. The y-axis do not start at 0, huge mistake when designing bar charts.
  2. Misleading title. Attempt to persuade the user that Netflix is failing in some way; or is an unsuccessful company
  3. The X and Y axis are not labeled, hard to determine what the values on the y-axis represent
  4. Year 2006 is highlighted with no clarification as to why. It's not the largest or smallest bar on the chart
  5. Tacitly omitting data from years after 2012 to show suggest continuing fall in closing price
  6. Data was consolidated to show the average closing price per year. Hard to identify monthly trends.

White Hat Visualization

Line Chart showing stock market closing price of Netfilx

Reason why it's not misleading and better..

  1. X and Y axis are clearly labeled showing what the values represent
  2. Title is unbiased, clear and brief title for what the chart is showing
  3. Line chart better represents upward/downward trends in the data
  4. All data from the dataset for years 2002-2022 are represented
  5. No consolidation of data

Itemized list of changes

  1. Changed bar chart into a line chart
  2. Renamed title
  3. Labeled X and Y axis
  4. Changed X-axis domain scale to time scale
  5. Set Y-axis to start at 0
  6. Uniform color for line path
