Pitchfork highest reviewed music genre

The last objective is to determine which music genre on Pitchfork's website received the most "good" reviews. This can be calculated by using aggregate data and finding the average review scores across each genre. In the early stages of the project I discovered that the average scores for each genre were close to equal. This made implementing ordered proportional symbols problamatic. I pivoted the visualization to an interactive treemap that includes a top-five list of the highest reviewed albums for each genre.

The treemap reveals Pitchfork reviewers tend to favor global music with an average review score of 7.35. Some notable albums that are included in the top-five list are Caetano Veloso: Livro, MIA: Kala, and Lee Scratch Perry: Ape-ology. The treemap also shows how close the average reviews scores are for each genre. The difference between the highest and lowest average score is only 0.45.
